Hi, I’m Ayush Saxena

I’m an aspiring Product Manager with a background in Computer Science (Data Science), driven by curiosity about how products influence user behaviour, decision-making, and real-world outcomes.

My interest in Product Management grew as I realized that real impact doesn’t come from building models alone — it comes from choosing the right problems, validating assumptions early, and connecting user needs with business outcomes.

My Journey: From Data Science to Product Thinking
  • Started with Data Science — learning how analytics and ML help measure outcomes
  • Realized the bigger challenge was deciding what problem to solve
  • Became more interested in user behavior, decision-making, and trade-offs
  • Transitioned toward Product Management — where data, users, and business meet
Building Product Thinking Through Hands-On Case Studies

Through hands-on case studies, I practiced core PM skills, including:

  • User research & problem discovery
  • Feature prioritization and trade-off analysis
  • Product analytics, funnels & retention strategy
  • ML-powered product thinking (value > accuracy)
